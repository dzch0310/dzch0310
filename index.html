Zichao Dong

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the ECOPRO research group in the Institute of Basic Sciences. I completed my PhD in Mathematics at Carnegie Mellon University in 2023, where I was very fortunate to be advised by Boris Bukh. I got my BsC in Mathematics at Peking University in 2018. Before that, I was an MOer at Beijing National Day School.

I mainly work on extremal problems from graph theory, discrete geometry, combinatorial number theory and other related fields, with an emphasis on understanding the interplay between algebraic, geometric, and probabilistic tools.
